Jeremiah 33:3

Call to me and I will answer you and tell you great and unsearchable things

you do not know.

Ask and You will tell me the secrets of the world.  What shall I ask?

Do I ask for the cure for cancer or pride?  

Do I ask how to become wealthy or secure?

No Father, none of that.

Father, if I could know something unknown, I would ask for this:

How can I represent you that people are drawn to you?

How can I have that deep love and self control that your Son had?

Father, your Son knew your word without hesitation, He prayed for sick people and they were healed. The deaf could hear, the blind could see and the demons fled.

The most knowledgeable men could not argue with him.

He brought love and laughter to all ages and to all people.

To serve you, to know the unknown is

a big cross to bear.  Honestly, I am not sure I am ready.  

So I ask Father, may I wake up each morning, as long as I have breath, with Jesus’ same love and strength in my heart, mind, and body.  May I never have too much of anything that I forget about you. May I never forget to glorify you in all things.

And the secret I ask for, how can I make you smile?
