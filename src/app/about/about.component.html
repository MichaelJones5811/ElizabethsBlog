About Me

There is not just one story that led to this blog, it is a combination of many stories.  For example, we had guest over for dinner. We asked one of the little girls visiting, will you say the blessing?  She did not know the prayer, God is Great, God is good…

 

Also, I have had moments in my life where the pain was so great, I didn’t know what to say.  I realized that we, the christian community, say all to often, “Pray about it, or I will pray about it” and we don’t know how or what to pray.

 

People aren’t born praying it takes direction and practise. There is no set magical way, it is your heart.  And It is my heart to share. As I have been given I will give back. Through the years, thankfully, I have learned to pray from friends, preachers, and the christian radio station.

 

So I share the prayers of my heart with you in hopes that your relationship with the Lord will flourish and grow stronger.

 

Elizabeth
