Guilt is a corroded mess we cannot contain.

 

Father, please let not guilt consume or separate you from my friend.  

In all the world and in all the love you have to share, I ask that you touch this man.  Heal him. Show him that the broken pieces are yours to carry.

 

As you gave Job back his strength, strengthen my friend.  May he breathe a fresh breath of air that fills his broken heart.

The world needs to hear and see your healing power.  May he be the one to march forth proclaiming it.

 

Jesus, look into my heart and see that I am in love with you.  Thank you for honoring me with this answered prayer.

 

In Jesus name I pray.

Amen and Amen
