John 16:11 the evil one stands condemned.

Oh what a Beautiful Day.  The air is cool and fresh; The birds are singing. Father, I long for you as I watch the sunrise and it's beauty and peace.  I have peace.

How can I ever thank you enough.  You accept each word I give and overlook the ones I should not give.

Father on this day, this most glorious day, remember your love for me and my family.

When the evil one enters your kingdom, the kingdom you created and own, please remember he and his angel stand condemned.  They are the ones that seek to destroy your pure love, your children and all that you created. They seek to destroy your heart father.

It is written ask and you shall receive.

So father on this day, this most glorious day, I pray I am filled even more with your Holy Spirit.  I ask that You do not hear the words of the evil one and his angels who stand condemned, but You hear my words of praise.  May my words echo in your ears and flow with Joy To Your Heart!

I ask that you remember no more what I've done to hurt you but you remember how you saved me.

How you chose.

You named me.

I am Yours.

Thank you.   Thank you Father.  Thank you.

 

In Jesus name I pray.   Amen and Amen.
