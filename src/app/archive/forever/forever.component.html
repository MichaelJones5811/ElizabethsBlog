Why would I accept anything that isn’t from you?

You are the most High God.

King of Eternity.  

Lover of all souls.

I refuse to accept or allow this prognosis or title in my life.  

I refuse to accept shame, guilt, and unforgiveness.

What I do accept is your forgiveness.

Your love.

Your healing.

I accept the title you have given me.  

Your crown jewel.  

Your heart’s desire.

Child of the Living God.

I could never repay you for your great sacrifice, but I will make every effort too.

For You are worthy of all honor.

You are worthy of all my praise, all my time, and all of my heart.

Forever.
