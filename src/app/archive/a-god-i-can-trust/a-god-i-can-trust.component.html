Isaiah 25 9

“Surely, you are my God. I trusted you and you saved me.”

You are my God.  You live today.

I am their target no more. Enough is enough.  It is your power that sustains me. You vigorously defend me. You are my protective wall they cannot scale.  When they charge me their feet crumble. They fall to their knees bowing to you O’ Great King. You silent their words and their breath is useless.  You are perfect faithfulness. You do marvelous things before my eyes. You remove my disgrace and dry my tears. Surely, you are the living God I can trust,  Without fail I will breathe again. You save me and my family. To you I give my heart and praise.

In Jesus name.  Amen and Amen
