Put an end to the lewdness.

Rebellious house, suffer the penalty and consequences of your sins no more.

The King of Glory has laid siege to evil today.

He brings forth an army of angels to stone and cut down that spirit of lewdness.

They hand over this mob that causes terror and plunder.

These scheming spirits will now be under the hand of Judgement.

God you have spoken.

It is time to act.

Do not hold back.

Do not take pity nor relent.

Clean the home, the city, and the nation of this spirit.

Now may your people repent.

May they keep their bodies and home clean.

May your people be free of anything which would cause them to stumble.

Clean and free their heart, minds, and body with the Blood of Jesus.
